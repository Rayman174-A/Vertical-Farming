Scheduled servicing of units


Ecofi has its customers in thoughts and prayers all the time, therefore, we make sure that members of our team visit you at accessible times of your choosing so that we can help you cultivate the best possible stock. These visits will ensure the wellbeing of the device and will also aid you to deliver your complaints face to face without any hindrance. Certain components and processes such as composting the soil and maintenance of the ecosystem require expert attention.

Trainings


Ecofi is also looking forward to beginning a series of training sessions with the motive of drawing light on simple yet effective ways to grow a garden in the confines of the urban society. Techniques like composting, managing kitchen waste and advanced gardening will be discussed and taught. Training sessions for kids will also be initiated, where values related to gardening and chomping all the vegies necessary for balanced diets will be inculcated in them. We will keep you all posted about the training session.
